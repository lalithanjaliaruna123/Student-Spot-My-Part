Our Vision

To become the leading platform where students can access everything they need for their tech education and career development - from learning resources and interview preparation to job opportunities and mentorship, all in one place.

Our Mission

To empower students with comprehensive tech resources, career opportunities, and a supportive community that bridges the gap between academic learning and professional success. We believe every student deserves access to quality resources and guidance to excel in their tech journey.

Core Values

Accessibility

Making quality tech education and resources available to all students, regardless of background.

Excellence

Curating only the highest quality resources and opportunities for our community.

Community

Building a supportive network where students can learn, grow, and succeed together.

Innovation

Continuously improving our platform to meet the evolving needs of tech students.

About the Founder

Rajkamal Panthagani

As a passionate advocate for student success in technology, Rajkamal founded Student Spot with the vision of creating a comprehensive platform that addresses the real challenges students face in their tech journey. With experience in both education and industry, he understands the gap between academic learning and professional requirements.

Through Student Spot, Rajkamal aims to democratize access to quality tech resources, career guidance, and professional networks, ensuring every student has the tools they need to succeed in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
